TARGET AUDIENCE

Site Plan - by Alex Giraldo


DESCRIPTION


The target audience is people wanting to visit the Space Coast, as well as current and potential business owners of the Space Coast.



PERSONA 1


Forty-seven year old David wants to expand his landscaping business into the Space Coast. He has the opportunity to partner with the Chamber of Commerce so that his business can get root in his new town. He is looking for connections, referrals, and to gain a good reputation in the community. Most of the time, he uses his desktop computer from home, and sometimes his mobile phone for internet access.



PERSONA 2


Frank, a German man in his late thirties, has planned a visit to the Space Coast to watch a rocket launch. The launch was delayed, and he is now wondering what to do in the meantime while he stays the extra two days in town, waiting. He is referred to the Chamber of Commerce website to look for places to eat, local crafts, and the businesses that rent kayaks and fishing equipment. He uses his mobile phone, and sometimes his tablet to access the internet.



SCENARIOS


  • What's going on in town this month?
  • I'm looking for a job. Who is hiring?
  • Where can I get some dinner tonight?
  • Is the nature preserve open right now?
  • Is there a lot of crime in the area? Safety guidance?
  • How do I expand my network with small businesses?
  • How to join the chamber of commerce?
  • What are the frequently asked questions about the town?
